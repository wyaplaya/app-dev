# Our Favorite Series: Money Heist (La Casa de Papel)

This repository is a group collaboration that demonstrates our knowledge of **Git workflow** and **Markdown formatting**. We chose *Money Heist* as our topic because it highlights the importance of strategy, teamwork, and planning—values that are essential in application development.

---

### Why our group loves this series:
* **Strategic Brilliance:** As engineering students, we admire how **The Professor** plans every detail and always has a Plan B.
* **Complex Characters:** The emotional depth of characters like *Berlin* and *Tokyo* makes the story unpredictable (Sean's favorite part).
* **Team Synergy:** It shows how people with different expertise can come together to achieve a "mission impossible" (Edgel's takeaway).

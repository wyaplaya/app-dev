# Our Favorite Series: Money Heist (La Casa de Papel)

This project is a collaboration between our group members to showcase our knowledge of Git workflow and Markdown. We chose **Money Heist** because we all appreciate its themes of strategy and teamwork.

### Why our group loves this series:
* **Strategic Brilliance:** As engineering students, we admire how **The Professor** plans every detail and always has a Plan B.
* **Complex Characters:** Characters like **Berlin** and **Tokyo** make the story unpredictable and emotional (Sean's favorite part).
* **Team Synergy:** It shows how people with different skills can come together to achieve a "mission impossible" (Edgel's takeaway).

### Favorite Quotes from the Heist:
> "To love, you need courage."
> 
> "Everything can go to hell in less than a second. In moments like this, you feel death approaching, and you know nothing will ever be the same."
